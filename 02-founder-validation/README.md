# Founder Validation

## Purpose

This folder is for proving that CESES is solving a real problem for real customers before investing heavily.

## Key Questions

- Who has the strongest need for CESES services?
- What problems do customers already pay to solve?
- What would make a customer trust CESES?
- What evidence shows the founder should continue, change, or stop an idea?

## Required Documents

- Founder assumptions list
- Customer interview notes
- Problem validation summary
- Early customer or partner contact list
- Lessons learned from each test

## Next Actions

- List the most important assumptions to test.
- Prepare simple customer interview questions.
- Interview at least 10 potential customers or partners.
- Summarize what was learned and what should change.
