# Finance Budget

## Purpose

This folder tracks CESES budgets, forecasts, funding needs, costs, and financial decisions.

## Key Questions

- How much money is needed to start and test the first offers?
- What are the monthly operating costs?
- What revenue is expected from pilots or services?
- What funding, grants, or investment may be needed?

## Required Documents

- Startup budget
- Monthly operating budget
- Cash flow forecast
- Pilot project budget
- Funding needs summary
- Financial assumptions list
