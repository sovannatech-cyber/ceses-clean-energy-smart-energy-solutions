# EV Charging

## Purpose

This folder is for exploring EV charging opportunities in Cambodia, including sites, partners, pricing, and pilot projects.

## Key Questions

- Where would EV chargers be most useful?
- Who owns or controls the best locations?
- What type of charger fits the market today?
- What pricing model could work for customers and site owners?

## Required Documents

- EV market notes
- Possible charging site list
- Partner and site owner contact list
- Charger options and supplier notes
- Pilot economics
- Regulatory or permitting notes

## Next Actions

- List possible early charging locations.
- Research charger suppliers and basic costs.
- Speak with site owners or fleet operators.
- Draft a small pilot concept.
